## 一、 美术风格基调 (Visual Style Guide)

为了凸显小说的“扮猪吃虎”、“复仇群像”与“黑深残”基调，建议生成图像时遵循以下美学标准：

*   **风格流派 (Art Genre)**：新中式写实水墨融合 (Cinematic Ink-wash Fusion / Dark Wuxia Noir)
*   **色彩矩阵 (Color Palette)**：
    *   **主色 (Dominant)**：炭黑 (`Charcoal Black`)、骨白 (`Bone White`)、苍灰 (`Muted Gray`)
    *   **辅助色 (Secondary)**：冷玉绿 (`Pale Jade`)、月白 (`Monthly-white` / `Pale blue-gray`)
    *   **点缀色 (Accent)**：朱砂红/血印 (`Cinnabar Red`)
*   **构图与光影**：采用低调强光影（Low-key Chiaroscuro）、大片留白与朦胧雾气，表现玄苍界压抑而厚重的氛围。

## 四、 世界观核心物件提示词 (World-Building Prop Prompts)

这些关键物件是小说中推动因果、算账和隐蔽真相的核心载体。可以使用以下提示词单独生成概念海报：

### 1. 《玄苍命册》（世界账册） (The World Fate Ledger)
*   **视觉特征**：厚重的古线装账册，泛黄的宣纸上画有墨色方格网，部分字迹闪烁着微弱的金色荧光，而有些名字则被彻底抹去（代表被隐蔽命格的阿湄等人）。
*   **提示词 (Prompt)**：
    > An atmospheric close-up of a heavy, ancient Chinese ledger book (Mingce) resting on a dark weathered wooden desk. The book is bound in aged, stitched hemp and open to reveal yellowed paper with vertical ink grids. Faint, glowing golden Chinese characters and mystical patterns seem to drift off the pages, with some names smudged or completely blank (erased destiny). Soft candlelight, rising incense smoke, moody wuxia style, watercolor textures. --ar 16:9 --style raw

### 2. 命运的凭证：素面竹牌与先祖玉牌 (Plaques of Destiny)
*   **视觉特征**：林夙的无字素面竹牌（修长、圆润、带指腹磨损感）与裴无咎的先祖古玉牌（带裂纹、雕刻着玄妙先祖纹路）并排搁在冰冷的雪石上。
*   **提示词 (Prompt)**：
    > A cinematic close-up shot of two crucial tokens resting on a cold, snow-dusted stone surface: a slender, plain, polished blank bamboo slip with no text, and a weathered, cracked ancient green jade plaque carved with faint ancestral patterns. Light snow dusts the stone. Moody low-key lighting, cool blue moonlight, high-texture realism with subtle ink-wash shading. --ar 16:9 --style raw

### 3. 未拆之信 (The Unopened Letter)
*   **视觉特征**：信封上写有苍劲的“叶”字，以朱砂红泥火漆封口，被一只纤细但指节发白的女手（苏挽）按在桌沿，没有拆开。
*   **提示词 (Prompt)**：
    > A close-up shot of a delicate, pale female hand gently pressing her fingers on the corner of an unopened ancient Chinese envelope resting on a dark desk. The envelope is sealed with red wax and has a bold, brush-written calligraphy character "叶" on it. Soft, warm lamp light, moody shadows, realistic skin and paper texture, cinematic suspense atmosphere. --ar 16:9 --style raw

## 五、 图像文件命名规范 (Image Naming Convention)

为了使项目文件整洁易读，建议生成的图片存入 `docs/assets/images/`，并采用以下格式命名：
*   **人物肖像**：`char_[姓名拼音].png` （例如：`char_linsu.png`, `char_yeguanlan.png`）
*   **群像剧照**：`still_[场景/描述].png` （例如：`still_group_poster.png`）
*   **世界观道具**：`prop_[道具名称拼音].png` （例如：`prop_mingce.png`, `prop_zhupai.png`）
